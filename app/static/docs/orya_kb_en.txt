=== Jewelry Care Guide ===

To maintain the brilliance and shine of your ORYA pieces for years to come, we recommend following these steps:

1. Avoid Chemicals: Keep jewelry away from perfumes, detergents, and creams, as these substances can affect the metal's luster.
2. Water & Humidity: It is best to remove jewelry before showering, swimming in pools (due to chlorine), or the sea.
3. Proper Storage: Store each piece in its own pouch or lined box to prevent friction and scratches.
4. Cleaning: Use a soft, dry cloth to gently wipe the piece after each use to remove skin oils or dust.
