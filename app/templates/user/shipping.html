Shipping & Delivery Policy

We are happy to deliver ORYA's sparkle to your doorstep across the Hashemite Kingdom of Jordan:

  • Delivery Areas: We ship to all governorates in Jordan (Amman, Irbid, Zarqa, Aqaba, Mafraq, Jerash, Karak, and more).
  • Delivery Time: Delivery takes between 48 to 72 business hours.
  • Shipping Fees: A flat shipping rate of 3 Jordanian Dinars (JOD) applies to all orders.
  • Inspection: You can inspect the pieces before receipt. If they do not meet your expectations, you only pay the delivery fee.
  • International Shipping: Currently, we do not offer shipping outside of Jordan.
